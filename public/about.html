About Me

Hey, I’m a 24-year-old guy from Iraq who’s really good at sitting still. How? Simple: movies, shows, and anime. I’ll watch anything that doesn’t put me to sleep—Hateful Eight and Django Unchained are my go-tos when I want to see people argue in cowboy hats. Inception and Interstellar are cool too, I guess, if you’re into "wait, what’s happening?" plots. Se7en? Great, but I’ll never look at a cardboard box the same way again.

When I’m not staring at screens, I’m either losing at video games (RPGs are my excuse for ignoring real life) or being ignored by my cat. Yes, I’m a cat person—dogs are too much work, and let’s be honest, cats are just tiny roommates who don’t pay rent.

Oh, and I’m "learning programming." Translation: I Google why my code doesn’t work for 5 hours a day. It’s fine.

One day I’ll make something useful, like an app that reminds me to stop rewatching The Office for the 10th time.

TL;DR: I like stuff, but not enough to tattoo it on my body
